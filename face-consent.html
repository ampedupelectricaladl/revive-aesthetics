Section 3

Quick health check

Answer honestly — a "yes" doesn't always mean you can't be treated, it just means Stefani will check in with you first.

Are you pregnant, or is there any chance you may be?
Are you currently breastfeeding?
Have you had facial surgery, or a cosmetic treatment on your face (microneedling, peel, laser), in the last 3 months?
Have you had anti-wrinkle injections or dermal filler in the last 4 weeks?
Are you prone to cold sores?
Do you have a blood clotting disorder, or take blood-thinning medication?
Any skin conditions, wounds or active breakouts on your face or neck?
Do you have epilepsy or light sensitivity, or take medication that makes skin light-sensitive?
Thanks for letting us know — Stefani will have a quick chat with you before your session to make sure everything is safe and comfortable for you. Nothing to worry about.
