Questions

Frequently asked

Everything you might want to know before your first visit to the studio.

What treatments does Revive Aesthetics offer in Adelaide?

Revive Aesthetics is a boutique skin clinic in Adelaide offering chemical peels, PDRN microneedling (collagen-induction skin needling) and LED light therapy. Every treatment begins with a personalised skin consultation, so your plan is built around your skin.

What is PDRN microneedling?

PDRN microneedling combines collagen-induction microneedling with a PDRN (polynucleotide) booster that supports your skin's natural repair. It helps soften scarring, smooth texture and improve firmness for a fresher, plumper complexion.

What can chemical peels help with?

Professional chemical peels resurface and brighten the skin — targeting dullness, congestion, uneven tone, pigmentation and the early signs of ageing. Peel strength is always tailored to your skin type and goals at your consultation.

Where are you located, and when are you open?

Revive Aesthetics is in Adelaide's city centre, open Monday and Tuesday, 9am – 9pm, by appointment. You can book online anytime, or call 0404 967 051.

Do I need a consultation before my first treatment?

Yes — every new client starts with a skin consultation. It lets us assess your skin, understand your goals and recommend the safest, most effective treatment plan for you.

How often should I have a treatment?

It depends on your skin and your goals, but most clients see the best results from a short course of treatments spaced a few weeks apart. Your therapist will map out a plan tailored to you at your consultation.
