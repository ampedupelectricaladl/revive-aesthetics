Pre-treatment form

A few questions so your treatment is safe and tailored to your skin. Takes about 2 minutes — everything is kept private.

Chemical peels and microneedling work with the surface of your skin, so this quick health check lets Stefani choose the right treatment and strength for you. If anything needs a closer look, she'll simply have a chat with you before your appointment.

Health & safety

Please answer honestly — this keeps your treatment safe. A "yes" doesn't always rule a treatment out; it just means Stefani will check a couple of things first.

Any active skin infection, open wound, sunburn or breakout in the treatment area right now?
Do you have diabetes, an autoimmune condition, immune suppression, or a bleeding/clotting disorder (or take blood thinners)?
